Dutint Pigment Preparations

Easily pourable pigment preparations which are highly concentrated and in paste form contaiing non-ionic and anionic wetting agenst and dispersing agents. These are Hydrophilic and mix readily into aqueous media to form stable dispersions. They arer miscible with one another in any ratio.


Their main application is the coloration of synthetic resin emulsion paints.
