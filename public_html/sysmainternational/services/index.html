Services

The SYSMA International Group serves two primary purposes:

1. To market raw materials for coating and polymer industries in North and South America.

o Export and Import raw materials for Coating and coating related Chemical industries.

2. To provide services to related organizations, primarily:

o Finding and setting up producers, in USA and overseas.

o Helping to resolve supply chain issues, including (but not limited to) warehousing
 transportation, distribution, shipping and receiving arrangements, and establishing supply net works.

The group consists of four individuals and four distributors in US as well as satellite offices in China and India.

I, Majid Reshamwala, have a masters degree in Polymer Science and have worked in coating industries for over 30 years. I have experience in developing products, running manufacturing operations and identifying outsourcing partners for polymer resin and paint industries. The product lines I have worked with are Alkyd, Polyester (Saturated and Unsaturated), Epoxy, Polyamide, Polyamine, Amino (Urea and Melamine formaldehyde) and Polyurethane resins.

I have worked both in paint and resin industries at multi-national companies such as Reichhold Chemicals, Mobil Oil and Poly Chrome. Armed with over 30 years of versatile experience, I have teamed up with experienced colleagues and acquaintances to form SYSMA International.

Ross Tucci , an experienced International Export businessman, overwatches our export sales to Central, South America and Puerto Rico.

Michael Eviston, an ISO 9000 series certification consultant, is responsible for our sales in the South Western United States. Micheal also takes charge of our regularity and quality operations.

Victor Rincon, a sales representative well versed in the technology of the chemical industry, is responsible for our sales in the North Eastern region of the United States. Victor has combined experience of over 25 years working for BASF and American Cyanamid.
